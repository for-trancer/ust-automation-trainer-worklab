# SCRUM MODEL

Requirements -> User Stories -> RPM(product owner)  -> PB -> SPM(scrum master) -> SB(development team) -> Start Sprint -> DSC(beginning of the day:avg 15 minutes) (IB) -> Sprint Done -> SR
-> SRF

- User Story

As a "role",I want to "perform a task",so that "achievement"

eg. As a "user",i want to  "login on ust page",so that "I can access all of its tools"

- Roles

1.Product Owner
2.Scrum Master
3.Development Team

- Meetings

1.Release Planning Meetings (RPM)
2.Scrum Planning Meeting (SPM)
3.Daily stand up call (DSC)
4.Sprint review (SR) (Whats done whats not done)
5.Sprint Retrospective (SRF) (Understanding team flow(adding or reducing story points))

- Backlogs

1.Product Backlog (PB)
2.Sprint Backlog (SB)
3.Impediment Backlog (IB : Challenges , Owned By Scrum Master)

- Estimation 

1.Planning Points(points = hours)
2.Story Points

- Daily Standup Meetings

1.Task you did yesterday?
2.Task you are going to do today?
3.Any challenges?

- Tools(Project Management)

1.Jira
2.Rally
3.Monday
